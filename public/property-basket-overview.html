How it fits together

Five roles. One continuous workflow.

Most property tools solve one slice and leave the hand-offs to email and WhatsApp. Property Basket connects every role, so an action in one dashboard instantly becomes the next person's to-do in theirs.

Agency

Lists, allocates, registers deals & pays out

→

Agent

Works leads, books viewings, closes

→

Tenant

Signs lease, pays rent, logs issues

→

Landlord

Sees portfolio, money & payouts

→

Contractor

Quotes, fixes, invoices & gets paid

One enquiry can travel the whole loop without leaving the platform — captured by the agent, registered by the agency, signed by the tenant, reported to the landlord, and resolved by a contractor.

The dashboards

A workspace built around what each role actually does.

01
Agency Dashboard

Command the whole agency

The control room for owners and managers — every agent, listing, deal and rand in one view, wrapped in the compliance South African agencies need.

  • Deal pipeline with a Registered stage — only the agency can move a closed deal to Registered, the moment commission is generated.
  • Listing allocation to agents, agent invitations and a full team roster.
  • Commission & payouts — separate sale & rental structures, per-agent splits, paid via Paystack or a captured bank account.
  • Maintenance control — allocate jobs, request & approve quotes, pay and rate contractors.
  • Compliance & trust — FFC/EAAB gating, Section 54 trust account, reports & billing.

Sits at the centre of everything: it feeds Agents listings & leads, releases their commission, surfaces the Tenant's maintenance requests, settles the Landlord, and dispatches & pays Contractors.

Agent Dashboard

Everything to close — and get paid

A focused workspace that turns enquiries into deals and tracks the agent's earnings at every step, with zero payout admin.

  • Listings, tenants, analytics & messaging, plus FFC reminders before they lapse.

End-to-end workflows

Three journeys that tie the dashboards together

Each step happens in the dashboard that owns it — and automatically becomes the next person's task, notification or payment.

Workflow A

Listing → Deal → Commission

  • 1
    Agency creates a listing and allocates it to an agent.Agency
  • 2
    Agent works the enquiry up the pipeline and closes the deal.Agent
  • 3
    Agency moves the closed deal to Registered — the trigger that generates commission.Agency
  • 4
    Commission appears in Commission & Payouts; the agent sees their split, read-only.Agent
  • 5
    Agency pays the agent via Paystack or their captured bank account.Agency
Workflow B

Lease → Rent → Settlement

  • 1
    Agent/Agency invites the tenant to the platform.Agency · Agent
  • 2
    Tenant reviews and digitally signs the lease (PDF generated).Tenant
  • 3
    Tenant pays rent via Paystack — or an automated debit order — and gets a receipt.Tenant
  • 4
    Payment reflects in the Agency trust flow and the Landlord's finances.Agency · Landlord
  • 5
    Landlord is settled to their captured banking details.Landlord
Workflow C

Maintenance → Repair → Payout

  • 1
    Tenant logs a maintenance request with photos.Tenant
  • 2
    Agency (and the agent, view-only) is notified and allocates a private or marketplace contractor.Agency
  • 3
    Contractor submits a quote; the job unlocks only once the agency approves it.Contractor · Agency
  • 4
    Contractor completes the job; the Tenant & Agency rate the work.Contractor · Tenant
  • 5
    The invoice lands in the Agency payout queue and the contractor is paid.Agency

Under the hood

Built for the South African market

The plumbing that makes every dashboard work together — compliant, automated and local by default.

Paystack payments

Rent collection, commission payouts & bulk transfers in ZAR, with webhooks and PDF receipts.

FFC / EAAB compliance

Agent & agency Fidelity Fund Certificates are enforced server-side, with expiry reminders.

Trust accounts & debit orders

Section 54 trust-account capture and tenant debit-order mandates, ready for audit.

Automated commission engine

Configurable sale & rental rates, per-agent splits and VAT handling — calculated on close.

PDF documents

Rent receipts, maintenance invoices, lease agreements & inspection reports, on demand.

Email notifications

Branded emails keep every party informed — invitations, payments, approvals & job updates.
