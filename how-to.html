Create an Agency account & invite an Agent

Register an estate agency, log in, and bring your first agent on board.

Video — Create an Agency account & invite an Agent Drop the recorded MP4 here (replace this box with a <video controls> tag).

PART A Register the agency

  1. Go to the Register screen and choose Estate Agency.
  2. Fill in your agency name, your name & phone.
  3. Email Agency@propertybasket.co.za, password PropertyAgency1! (×2), and your EAAB FFC number.
  4. Tick the terms box and click Create account & start free trial.
  5. Choose a plan (or apply a promo code) to activate the dashboard.
  6. A welcome / verification email is sent to the agency inbox.
  7. Open Sign in, enter the agency email & password → land on the Agency dashboard.

PART B Invite an agent

  1. In the dashboard, open Agents (or click Invite Agent).
  2. Enter the agent's name and email Agent@propertybasket.co.za, then send the invite.
  3. The agent receives an invitation email with an activation link.
  4. They open the link, set the password PropertyAgent1! and accept.
  5. The agent signs in at Sign in and lands on the Agent dashboard.
Note on email: in this environment mail is written to the log, so the verification & invitation emails appear in storage/logs/laravel.log (in production they're delivered to the inbox). The activation link in the invite is what the agent clicks to set their password.
